Google Developer Days

Creating live experiences that inspire, engage, and educate developers

Google Developer Days (GDD) are global events showcasing the latest developer products and platforms from Google to help you quickly develop high quality apps, grow & retain an active user base, and tap into tools to earn more.

The Dev Summit program was designed and created by GDS Events to produce and execute live experiences. These tentpole events are where we connect developers and their engaging community in a physical place.

Defining objectives and tracking performance

We have a set of best practices and templates that we follow and build on event over event, and we use these tools to offload the grunt work: this frees you to focus on your narrative and OKRs for your PA.

We handle the following tasks for events:

  • Defining objectives and key performance indicators
  • Budgeting and resource management
  • Conference agenda planning and speaker support
  • Coordination and logistics
  • Website direction and technical support
  • Registration and attendee communications
  • Day-to-day project management
  • Stage direction and production planning for speakers
  • Onsite event production and staffing
  • Post-event analysis and reporting

Can't forget the after party...

We turn the event into a sociable and entertaining experience for all attendees.
