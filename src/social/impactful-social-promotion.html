Impactful social promotion

Social on a mission

Our focused social-media campaigns educate, inspire, and inform developers who build products with Google’s technologies. We manage all aspects of social promotions for various social channels as a part of Google Developers Studio.

Anticipating Android Foldables as a new form factor coming next year from partners

In collaboration with the Android DevRel and Marketing teams, GDS Social delivered a breaking news piece to the developer community during the Android Dev Summit 2018 keynote. The ‘foldables’ tweet garnered a DevRel record breaking 1.13M impressions, 259K media views, 57K engagements, and 5% engagement rate. This announcement tweet caused a visible spike in viewers on the livestream, further exemplifying the value of integrated social promotions.

Making strides for EDII

Pride 2019 was one of the biggest in recent history as cities around the world celebrated the 50th anniversary of the Stonewall Riots, which helped catalyze LGBTQIA+ movements globally. To celebrate and honor developers from the LGBTQIA+ community on this important occasion, we launched a multi-platform campaign that highlighted the achievements and opinions of a few individuals who are making strides toward a more diverse future in tech. Each of the six individual posts was presented as a continuous slide-carousel on Instagram and as a click-through story that now sits on our profile page as a highlight. The activation was also promoted on Twitter and Facebook.
