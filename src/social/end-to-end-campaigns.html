End to End Campaigns

Crafting a relatable developer experience

We work with Google’s developer product areas to craft meaningful social campaigns that deliver fun, engaging, and personalized experiences across our suite of properties. Our campaigns run the gamut, from something as simple as driving awareness around a hashtag to working cross-functionally with the various teams in Google Developer Studio team.

Gamifying Announcements

To surprise and delight our followers in the lead-up to last year’s Android Dev Summit, we collaborated with PAs across GDS to send fans on a social scavenger hunt for pieces of a puzzle that would reveal exclusive 10-Year-Anniversary artwork by “Silicon Valley” artist, Garson Yu. Fans had a blast hunting down the pieces and some even put it together before the reveal. Team work truly does make the dream work.
