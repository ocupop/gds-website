YouTube channel management

Our team of YouTube experts stays on top of the video-sharing platform’s changing landscape. We influence the entire video lifecycle of video in Google Developers Studio, from writing SEO best practices, moderating comments, and analyzing and reporting on smart YouTube statistics to working directly with our content creators and producers.

Psychological safety for our developer communities.

GDS values a commenting culture that is abuse-free and conducive to our overall mission of educating, inspiring and informing developers. We have established a robust set of policies that addresses 25 categories of abusive comments and are continuously expanding as we learn more about the community’s needs.

Reporting insights and analysis

Our vast mine of data determines the directions and risks that we take. We handle all the metrics for Google Developer Studio and its partners, from compiling weekly reports on every video to crafting custom reports for any particular need.
