Google Developer swag

The physical manifestation of Google’s developer brands

Swag provides developers with something that they want to flaunt, making for an easy and cost-effective way of boosting brand awareness and spreading our message.

External and internal events

We develop swag for external and internal events, including the Google Video Summit, Google’s DevRelCon, and multiple I/O after parties. We also create tools and assets that allow our dev community leaders to throw great events and scale their outreach.

Inclusive design

We produce all swag for our developer communications, which enables us to coordinate the needs and wants of product teams and content creators. We provide internal guidelines and checkpoints for swag creation to ensure that anyone can meaningfully interact with our products.

Interactive swag

Swag can teach developer concepts and approaches to help developers adhere to best practices.
