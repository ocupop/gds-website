Google Developers’ Brand Visuals

A refreshed brand for Google Developers

We refreshed the Google Developers brand and applied it across Developer Relations ecosystem of programs, including events, websites, training, social media profiles, and developer programs.

Increased brand awareness through a consistent visual story

Prior to 2019, Google’s wide array of developer offerings went largely unrecognized by developers, sowe set out to create an undeniably Google developer brand system that we could apply to multiple programs and product areas.

Undeniably Google

We iterated on more than 20 visual and naming systems for potential identity solutions. Very early in the process, we found that using the 4-color Google logo tested the strongest in the undeniably Google category. Upon testing multiple naming options for the brand, we found that the current name—Google Developers —already yielded good equity in our community programs. We made the data-backed decision to move forward with the Google Developers word mark using the 4-color Google logo.

Developer aesthetic

We developed art guidelines that reference grid paper and geometry commonly seen in engineering culture. We also relied heavily on photography to communicate Google’s values, including inclusivity, empowerment, community—the primary imagery in our marketing materials etc.

Globally deployed

We led an approval process and launch plan in collaboration with the Developer Marketing Team to deploy the new system throughout our ecosystem in April 2019. The launch plan required coordination with the leads of multiple developer product and ecosystem teams, and the regional leads of our localized properties.
