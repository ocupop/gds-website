Content for Events

We create topic pages that curate content that's relevant before, during, and after events. For example, we created a topic page that showcased Firebase leading up to the Firebase Summit in 2019, which featured codelabs, experts and influencers, news articles, and videos. During the summit we unveiled a new codelab immediately after the keynote in accordance with the timing requested by the Firebase team. Today we maintain the page as an evergreen source of information about Firebase.

Editing

We handle editorial work, such as the need to edit new codelabs for the first time and existing codelabs that haven't been edited in a long time. Our technical writers ensure that the codelabs abide by Google's style guidelines for developer documentation and work with you to capture accurate technical instructions that developers can easily follow. For example, we're working with the Android team to edit their codelabs for Google I/O 2020, therefore alleviating some of the massive amount of work that they need to do before the conference.

Content for campaigns

We create topic pages that curate content specific to campaigns that are aimed at specific developer groups. For example, we created a topic page for International Women's Day in 2020, which featured many successful women throughout Google and their advice for aspiring female developers.
